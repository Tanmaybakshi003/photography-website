Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 June 2026

Arun Sareen Photography ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store and share information when you visit our website, submit a booking inquiry, or engage our photography and videography services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact details you provide via our booking inquiry form — name, email address, phone number, event date, service required and any details you share about your project.
  • Communication records from emails, WhatsApp messages, or calls related to your inquiry or booking.
  • Photographs and video footage captured during a shoot you have booked with us, including images of you, your family, guests or staff where applicable.
  • Technical data such as IP address, browser type and pages visited, collected automatically through standard website analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Respond to booking inquiries and provide quotes for our services.
  • Communicate with you about your shoot, schedule, deliverables and invoices.
  • Deliver edited photographs, films and related files via private galleries or file-sharing links.
  • Improve our website, services and customer experience.
  • With your consent, showcase select images in our portfolio, website gallery, social media or marketing materials.

3. Use of Images for Portfolio & Marketing

We may wish to feature images or footage from your shoot in our portfolio, website, social media (including Instagram) or promotional materials. We will only do so with your prior consent, which may be requested as part of your booking agreement. You may opt out of such usage at any time by notifying us in writing at hello@arunsareenphotography.com.

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate our business, such as:

  • Form processing services (e.g. Formspree) used to receive booking inquiries.
  • Cloud storage and gallery delivery platforms used to share edited photos and films.
  • Payment processors for invoicing and advance payments.

These providers are only given access to information necessary to perform their function and are required to handle it securely.

5. Data Retention

We retain booking inquiry details and communication records for as long as necessary to fulfil our services and for reasonable record-keeping (typically up to 3 years). Photographs and video footage from completed projects may be retained as part of our portfolio archive unless you request deletion.

6. Your Rights

Depending on applicable law, including India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal and contractual obligations.
  • Withdraw consent for use of your images in our portfolio or marketing.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@arunsareenphotography.com.

7. Cookies & Analytics

Our website may use cookies or similar technologies to understand how visitors use the site and to improve performance. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings, though some site features may not function correctly without them.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, loss or misuse. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services, including family, baby and maternity photography, may involve photographing minors with the consent and presence of their parents or guardians. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children without parental consent.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when it was last revised.
